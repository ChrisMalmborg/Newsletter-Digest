Fwd: Mozi Minute: Scaling Your Sales Script

Alex Hormozi explains how to create scalable sales scripts using the CLOSER framework by documenting 50+ founder calls, simplifying to under 500 words, and focusing on process adherence over individual selling styles.

• Sales scripts should be created from 50+ calls by founders/top sellers, then structured using the CLOSER framework (Clarify, Label, Overview, Sell, Explain, Reinforce) to standardize high-converting conversations.
• Effective sales scripts should be under 500 words (4 minutes of talking) and written at a 3rd grade reading level to maximize adherence and comprehension.
• AI tools can be used to analyze call recordings and help draft initial sales scripts, streamlining the documentation process.
• Treating sales as a repeatable process rather than an art enables predictable close rates, faster rep ramp-up, and easier identification of what to fix when performance drops.
• The key is avoiding unscalable tactics like founder authority/expertise and instead replicating what converts best for reps without those advantages.
