Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What data we collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • • Email address — your Gmail address, used to identify your account and deliver your daily digest.
  • • OAuth tokens — read-only access tokens issued by Google, used to fetch newsletter emails from your inbox. We never store your Gmail password.
  • • Newsletter preferences — the list of newsletter senders you have chosen to include in your digest.

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the TLDRead service:

  • • Fetching newsletter emails from your Gmail inbox using read-only OAuth access
  • • Generating AI-powered summaries via the Anthropic Claude API
  • • Sending your daily digest email to your registered address

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond delivering your digest.

Data storage

Your account data is stored securely on our servers. We retain your OAuth tokens only as long as your account is active. Newsletter content processed to generate summaries is not permanently stored. We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing privacy@newsletterdigest.app. We will process deletion requests within 30 days. You can also revoke Gmail access at any time from your Google account permissions.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate TLDRead:

  • • Google OAuth & Gmail API — for authenticating your account and reading newsletter emails with read-only access. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • • Anthropic Claude API — for generating AI summaries of your newsletters. Email content sent for summarization is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at privacy@newsletterdigest.app.
